    Here are a couple more things that make me, ME! As I've designed for others and their visions, I've learned more about who I am!

  1. Flowers!
    • My favorite flowers are English Lavendar flowers! Partly because Lavendar is my favorite color, but they are also so simple and elegant in any occasion.
    • I love putting up different bouquets of flowers around my home. It brightens up any room!
    • I grew up in Utah and would look forward to watching the flowers bloom in the canyon each year!
  2. Tennis!
    • I have played tennis since I was 11 years old and have loved it ever since!
    • I played all 4 years of high school and would play year round at an indoor club at the heart of Salt Lake City, Utah.
    • Tennis will always have a place in my heart because it not only brought me lifelong friends, but a hobby that brings me JOY.
    • My all time favorite player to watch was Roger Federer!!
  3. Mountains!
    • The outdoors have always been an escape for me and one of my favorite things to do is hiking!
    • Zions National Park is one of the best! I try to go there at least once a year.
